Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers about coverage terms, registration, claims, and how to contact support for your Heat & Cool product protection plan.

What does the CPS extended warranty cover?
Most plans cover parts and labor for mechanical and electrical failures after the manufacturer warranty period, based on your specific contract terms.
When does my coverage start?
Coverage timing depends on your plan. Some protection begins on the purchase date, while extended portions continue after the manufacturer warranty expires.
How do I register my product warranty?
Use the registration option on the website and provide your product details, proof of purchase, and contact information. Keep the confirmation email for your records.
How do I file a claim?
Go to the claim section, submit your plan and product details, and describe the issue. A claims specialist will guide you through the next steps.
Do I need to keep my receipt and plan documents?
Yes. Keep your purchase receipt, warranty registration confirmation, and service contract details to speed up claim validation.
Are accessories and additional parts included?
Many plans include accessory and component protection, but coverage limits vary. Review your policy terms for exact inclusions and exclusions.
Can I transfer my warranty plan to someone else?
Some plans are transferable. Contact support with your plan number and transfer request to confirm eligibility and required documentation.
How do I contact support?
Reach support by phone at (800) 905-0443, internationally at +1 (347) 535-3518, or by email at cs@cpscentral.com.
